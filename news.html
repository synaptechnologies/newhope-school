Admissions Now Open for 2024-2025 Academic Year

November 15, 2023 Admin

We are pleased to announce that admissions for the 2024-2025 academic year are now open at New Hope School. We invite parents to apply for places in our Nursery, Primary, and Junior High School programs.

At New Hope School, we are committed to providing quality education with a focus on discipline, knowledge, and service. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to create a nurturing environment where students can excel academically and develop strong character.

Spaces are limited, so we encourage early applications to secure a place for your child. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that both parents and students have a clear understanding of what to expect.
