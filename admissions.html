Join the New Hope Family

Thank you for considering New Hope School for your child's education. We are delighted that you are interested in becoming part of our community.

Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that both parents and students have a clear understanding of what to expect. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and believe that every child brings unique strengths to our school community.

At New Hope School, we are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our dedicated faculty and staff are here to support your child's educational journey.

Admissions Process

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to inquire about available spaces and schedule a school tour. You can call us at 0244523510 or email us at info@newhopeschool.

2

School Tour

Visit our campus to experience our learning environment firsthand. During the tour, you'll have the opportunity to meet our faculty, see our facilities, and learn more about our programs.

3

Application

Complete the application form and submit all required documents. You can fill out the online application form below or obtain a physical copy from our admissions office.

4

Assessment

Prospective students will undergo a simple assessment to determine their academic readiness. This helps us ensure that we can provide the appropriate support for your child's educational needs.

5

Interview

Both parents and students will have a brief interview with a member of our admissions team. This is an opportunity for us to get to know your family better and for you to ask any questions you may have.

6

Admission Decision

After completing all the steps, you will receive an admission decision within one week. If accepted, you will receive an offer letter with instructions on how to complete the enrollment process.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or passport
  • Previous school reports/transcripts (for transfer students)
  • Immunization records
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Parent/Guardian identification

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: 2-4 years
  • Kindergarten: 4-6 years
  • Class 1: 6 years
  • Class 2-6: Age-appropriate for grade level
  • JHS 1-3: Completion of primary education

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

Applications are accepted throughout the year, but we recommend applying at least three months before the start of the academic year for the best chance of securing a place. The academic year begins in September.

Is there an entrance examination?

Yes, prospective students are required to take a simple assessment to determine their academic readiness. The assessment covers basic skills in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge appropriate for their age level.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer a limited number of scholarships based on academic merit and financial need. Scholarship applications are typically due two months before the start of the academic year. Please contact our admissions office for more information.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention. In Nursery and Kindergarten, the ratio is 15:1. In Primary classes, it's 20:1, and in JHS, it's 25:1.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes, we provide transportation services for students living within a 10km radius of the school. There is an additional fee for this service. Please contact our admissions office for routes and fees.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, athletics), clubs (science, drama, debate, coding), music, and art. These activities are designed to complement our academic program and promote holistic development.
